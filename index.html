Our Unique Bee Keeping Course

Introduction to Bee-Keeping: this one-day course is designed as an introduction for potential beekeepers with less than 2 years of experience.

The morning is a classroom lecture, while the afternoon is a practical demonstration in our hives to allow you "hands on" experience. We will cover all aspects of what you should be looking for at your hive inspection so that you understand the life cycle and learn to "read" your bees. Protective clothing will be supplied if required but please bring stout boots or "wellies" and a packed lunch. For further information please click here!

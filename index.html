Our Unique Beekeeping Course

Introduction to beekeeping: this one-day course is designed as an introduction for potential beekeepers with less than 2 years of experience.

The morning is a classroom lecture, while the afternoon is a practical demonstration in our hives to allow you "hands on" experience. We will cover all aspects of hive inspection so that you understand and learn to "read" your bees. Protective clothing will be supplied if required although bring boots if possible.

Save the bees and our environment! "Bee Cool"! Visit Scotland!

Queen Bee is proud of its vision and commitment to sustainability and local supply chain here in Melrose, Scotland. As we mentioned before, all our products are locally sourced and handmade. We use recyclable products for serving, wrapping and storing because we believe this is the only way to save our planet.

There is no plan "BEE". Bees are taking cruical part in fertilisation of many crops. There are around 70 types of them in the UK that depend or benefit from bee pollination. It is well known it would cost UK farmers a fortune to pollinate their crops manually if bees disappeared. Let's save them together!
